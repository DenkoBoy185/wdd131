Overview

Site Name

Zero-G Nexus

Reason (Realistic Approach)

The manipulation of gravity represents the next great frontier in aerospace engineering and deep space exploration. I chose this subject to create a serious, credible digital resource that explores the bridge between current theoretical physics (like Alcubierre drives or diamagnetic levitation) and near-future technological applications. This project challenges me to present complex scientific concepts in an accessible, structured web format.

Purpose (Academic/Technical Purpose)

Zero-G Nexus serves as a speculative research hub dedicated to advanced propulsion and gravity modification theories. Unlike fantasy-based sites, its purpose is to analyze the scientific viability of "anti-gravity." It aims to educate users on real-world physics constraints and showcase grounded, scientifically plausible concepts for future infrastructure, vehicles, and energy systems.

Audience

The primary audience includes engineering students, aerospace enthusiasts, hard sci-fi writers seeking technical accuracy, and people interested in futurism and space settlement. They value plausible data and realistic visuals over pure fantasy trope.

Scenarios (Realistic User Stories)

1. "As an aerospace engineering student, I want to find a clear explanation of the theoretical energy requirements for a warp field."

2. "As a hard sci-fi author, I need to research plausible mechanisms for artificial gravity on rotating space stations to ensure my novel is scientifically accurate."

3. "As a futurist enthusiast, I want to see realistic concept designs of near-future orbital construction platforms utilizing zero-g manufacturing."
